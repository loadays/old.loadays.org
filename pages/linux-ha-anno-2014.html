Linux HA, anno 2014

abstract

While High Availability tools are known and proven technology, there are some moves made by the actors around it in the past years.

This talk will give an introduction to Linux HA, explaining the key concepts, what has changed and in which directions we are going.

bio

Julien Pivotto is a young Open-Source consultant at Inuits where he is helping organisations with the deployment of long-term solutions based on Open-source infrastructure.

He is a strong believer in the devops movement and has technical focus towards infrastructure automation, continuous integration and monitoring.
